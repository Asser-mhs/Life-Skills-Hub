Time Management Tips

Plan Your Day

Write down tasks in the morning or the night before. Knowing what to do helps you stay focused.

Prioritize

Do the most important tasks first. Not everything on your list has the same importance.

Break Tasks Down

Big projects feel easier when split into smaller steps. Reward yourself after finishing each one.

Avoid Distractions

Turn off notifications or find a quiet place to work so you can use your time better.
